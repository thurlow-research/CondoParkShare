# User Guide

A guide for Bellevue Towers residents using BT ParkShare.

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Go to the site and click **"Need an account? Register"**
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Full Name** — Your name as neighbors will see it
   - **Email Address** — Used for login and notifications
   - **Password** — Minimum 8 characters (enter twice to confirm)
   - **Unit Number** — Your unit at Bellevue Towers
   - **Parking Spot #** — Your assigned parking spot number
   - **Phone** — Contact number (shared with people using your spot)
3. Click **Register**

Your account must be approved by the site administrator before you can log in. You'll receive an email when approved.

### Logging In

Go to the login page and enter your email and password. After login you'll see the **Dashboard**.

## Dashboard

The dashboard shows four stat cards at a glance:

- **My Shared Spots** — How many of your parking donations are currently active
- **My Spots In Use** — How many of your shared spots are currently being used by someone
- **My Reservations** — How many spots you've reserved from others
- **Available Now** — How many spots are available right now from other residents

Below the stats are quick-action buttons to **Share My Spot** or **Find a Spot**.

## Sharing Your Parking Spot

When you'll be away and your parking spot will be empty, you can make it available for other residents' guests.

1. Click **Share My Spot** in the navigation or dashboard
2. Set the **Available From** date and time (when your spot becomes free)
3. Set the **Available Until** date and time (when you need it back)
4. Click **Share My Spot**

Your spot will appear in search results for other residents during that time window.

### Managing Your Shared Spots

Go to **My Shared Spots** to see all your donations. From here you can:

- **Edit** — Change the start or end time (only for available, non-expired spots)
- **Cancel** — Remove the spot from availability
- **See who's using it** — If someone has reserved your spot, you'll see their name, unit, phone number, and the time window they reserved

## Finding a Parking Spot

When you need a guest parking spot:

1. Click **Find a Spot** in the navigation or dashboard
2. Enter the **start date/time** you need the spot
3. Enter the **end date/time** you need the spot
4. Click **Search**

You'll see a list of available spots that cover your entire requested time window. Each result shows the spot number and who shared it.

### Reserving a Spot

1. Click **Reserve** next to an available spot
2. Review the confirmation details (spot number, donor, time range)
3. Click **Confirm Reservation**

Both you and the spot owner will receive email confirmations with each other's contact info.

**Time splitting:** If the shared spot covers more time than you need, the remaining time automatically stays available for others. For example, if someone shared their spot for the entire weekend and you only need Saturday, Sunday remains available for other residents.

### Managing Your Reservations

Go to **My Reservations** to see all spots you've reserved. You can:

- View donor contact info (name, unit, phone, email)
- **Cancel** an active reservation if plans change — the spot returns to the available pool

## Email Notifications

You'll receive emails for:

- **Account approved** — When the admin approves your registration
- **Spot reserved (as donor)** — When someone reserves your shared spot, including their contact info
- **Spot confirmed (as borrower)** — When you successfully reserve a spot, including the donor's contact info
- **Reservation expired** — A reminder when your reservation time has ended

## Tips

- Share your spot whenever you're away — even short trips help!
- Search with flexible times to see more options
- Check the dashboard regularly for available spots during busy periods
- Keep your phone number current so neighbors can reach you about parking
