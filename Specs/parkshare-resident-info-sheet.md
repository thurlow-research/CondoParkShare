# CondoParkShare — Information for Residents

*Share a parking spot. Find one when you need it. Here's how it works.*

---

## What it is

CondoParkShare is a simple way for neighbors in your building to share parking. When you're away and your spot sits empty, you can offer it. When you need parking your own spot can't cover — a visitor, a second car, a maintenance day — you can book a neighbor's open spot by the hour.

## How to use it

**When you're away — list your spot**
- Open the app, tap **"I'm away,"** pick your dates.
- Your spot is now available for neighbors to book, hour by hour, for just the times you're gone.
- You can list a recurring pattern too (for example, every weekday while you're at work).

**When you need parking — book a spot**
- Search the **time you need** ("Tuesday 9 am–noon").
- The app shows which spots are free then. Pick one, confirm, done.
- You'll get a confirmation, and a reminder before your time is up.

## A nice perk for sharing

The more you list your own spot, the **further ahead you're allowed to book** others' spots. It's a simple fairness feature: people who help keep the pool full get first pick of it. Nothing to track — it just happens automatically based on how much you share.

## What it costs you

That depends on how your building set it up:

- **Free** — many buildings run it at no cost to residents.
- **Building-paid** — your HOA covers it; you pay nothing and book freely.
- **Small monthly fee** — in some buildings, residents who want the service pay a small flat fee.

Either way, **everyone starts with a free trial period** (usually 90 days) so you can try it before anything is charged — and if a fee ever applies, you'll be told clearly in advance and can cancel anytime during the trial at no cost.

## Getting started

- You'll receive an **invite link** from your building (CondoParkShare is private to your community — only residents can join).
- Set up your account, turn on **two-factor authentication** with an authenticator app (keeps your account secure), and save your backup codes.
- That's it — list a spot or book one whenever you like.

## Your privacy

- Your account is protected with two-factor sign-in.
- Your personal details are encrypted and only used to run the service.
- You can ask to have your information deleted at any time.
- Your building's CondoParkShare is private to your building.

## A few good-neighbor notes

- **Book what you actually need.** You hold one booking at a time, so reserve the hours you'll really use and free the spot when you're done — someone else may need it.
- **Heading back early?** Release your booking so the spot returns to the pool.
- **Listed your spot but plans changed?** You can cancel, but try to give notice — a neighbor may be counting on it.

## In one line

*List your spot when you're away, grab a neighbor's when you need one — easy, fair, and private to your building.*
